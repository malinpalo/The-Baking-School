Baking can be easy and fun, join us on a class to learn how!

Why bake?

  • Baking your own bread, your own cake will make you feel satisfaction.
  • There's notting like the smell of fresh baked bread, straight from the oven.
  • Impress your grandma, the neighbour, your kids.

How does It work?

  • We'll meet up at our fully equipped school kitchen.
  • You will get a short introduction to the art of baking, some handy tips and a walkthrough of the recipe.
  • There we will supply you with a recipe, all the baking tools and ingredients needed.
  • Together we bake and when everything is out of the oven we sit down to have a taste of the creation together with a cup of tea or coffee.
